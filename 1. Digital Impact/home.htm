With the development of technology digital devices. (smart phones, smart watches, tablet, video game consoles, televisions and computers) appear in every aspect of children lives. Halpert claimed that “92% of children play games” on smart phones when they were two, and “1/3 of children own a full feature tablet” in United States (2015). Studies show negative impacts, such as kids's creativity, imagination and development, are gowning since they start using smart devices early (Straker at al., 2010). The use of digital devices is growing and making negative effects on children’s health. Thus, children, especially those under ten, shouldn’t use or own digital devices.
